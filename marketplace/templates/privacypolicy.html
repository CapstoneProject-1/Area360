Privacy Policy

At Area360, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our clients and customers. We understand that when you provide us with personal information, you trust us to handle it with care and respect. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you visit our website or use our services.


Collection of Information

We collect information about you in various ways, including:

Information you provide directly to us: We collect information that you provide to us directly, such as your name, contact information, and other personal or financial information when you register for an account, request information, or make a purchase.

Information we collect automatically: When you visit our website or use our services, we may collect certain information automatically, including your IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, and location data. We may also collect information about your interactions with our website and services, such as pages visited and links clicked.

Information we collect from third parties: We may also collect information about you from third parties, such as public databases, social media platforms, and other partners. For example, if you sign in to our website using your Facebook account, we may collect information from Facebook about your name, profile picture, and email address.


Use of Information

We use the information we collect about you to provide, maintain, and improve our services, as well as to communicate with you and personalize your experience. Some specific ways we may use your information include:

To create and manage your account: We use your information to create and manage your account, including verifying your identity and processing transactions.

To provide customer support: We use your information to troubleshoot issues and provide assistance when you contact us for support.

To send marketing communications: If you opt in to receive marketing communications from us, we may use your information to send you updates about our services, promotions, and other marketing messages. You can opt out of receiving these communications at any time by following the unsubscribe instructions included in the message.

To analyze and improve our services: We may use your information to analyze and improve our website and services, including by conducting research and analytics, testing new features, and identifying trends and patterns.


Sharing of Information

We may share your information with third parties in certain circumstances, including:

With our service providers: We may share your information with third parties who provide services to us, such as website hosting, data analysis, payment processing, and customer service. These service providers are required to keep your information confidential and use it only to perform the services they are contracted to provide.

For legal purposes: We may disclose your information if we are required to do so by law or in response to a subpoena, court order, or other legal process. We may also disclose your information to prevent or investigate a potential crime, such as fraud or identity theft.

In connection with a sale or merger: If we sell or merge our business with another company, we may share your information as part of that transaction. In such cases, we will require the acquiring company to follow this Privacy Policy with respect to your information.


Security of Information

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. These measures include using encryption and secure servers, as well as regularly monitoring and reviewing our security practices.

However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is completely secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
